About me

After finishing my bachelor degree at Paris Descartes University, I decided to continue my studies at EPITA, an engineering school. The first year allowed me to develop my software development skills via multiple projects.

It was during my 4 month internship, at enioka, a small consulting company based in Paris, that allowed me to discover « big system » dedicated to entreprises. Having particularly enjoyed this experience, I chose systems architecture as my speciality for the last year of my engineering studies. I have strengthened my knowledge in this field through projects focusing on architecture, infrastructure and virtualization in conjonction with softskills such as presenting, communication, persuasion and leadership.

My end of studies internship at Airbus Defence and Space in Munich is a good opportunity for me to apply what I learnt, from theoretical design to practical implementation.
